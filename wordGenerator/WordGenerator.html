Word Generator

Generates random "realistic" words based on language statistics.




Triplets Pairs
info Language statistics are built by parsing list of words and storing probabilities for pairs (letter 1 followed by letter 2) and triplets (letter 1 followed by letter 2 followed by letter 3).

Triplets algorithm: the next letter is selected based on triplets probabilities.

Pairs algorithm: N letters are pre-selected based on pairs probabilities from which the letter maximizing triplets probability is selected.

N=1 means triplets probabilities are not used.
Lower N values generate more creative but less realistic words.
